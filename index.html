Pella Tulips

Every year at the beginning of May in Pella, Iowa hundreds of tulips come into bloom. Tulips of all colors can be seen all over town. During this time many people come to visit the town of Pella, Iowa.

Tulip Time Festival

Every year the Tulip Time Festival is held in Pella, Iowa. This festival consists of a bunch of different activities that take place over three days. The activities are a parade, mueseums and tours, gardens, performances, and displays.
