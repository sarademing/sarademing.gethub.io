Serena Williams Beats Robot to Win the Australian Open

Serena Williams faces off against a robot in the finals of the Australian Open. Serena won the match two sets to one. Serena took the first set of the match 6-3, and the robot rallied back in the second set to win 6-4. However, Serena did not let that get her down, because she came back and won the third set 6-1. Serena has become the first tennis player to defeat a robot in the final of a major.

Simone Biles Wins her Sixth Straight Olympic All Around Title

Simone Biles has won the 2036 Olympic gymnastics all around title. This is Simone's sixth straight Olympic Games, and she has won every single Olympic Games that she has been to. Simone is now 40 years old, which makes her the oldest gymnast to ever win a medal at the Olympic Games.
